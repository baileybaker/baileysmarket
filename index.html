How We Work

Seed to table.
Nothing extra.

Every product is grown, processed, and packed here — no middlemen, no outsourced ingredients. This business is evolving and shaped around a core committment to quality ingredients and materials you can trust.

01

Zero Plastic

Product is packed in glass, kraft, or compostable packaging. Food-grade PLA and PET only when necessary.

02

Chemical Free

No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. Managed with cultivation, rotation, and timing.

03

Honestly Labeled

What's on the label is what's in the jar. Grown and produced locally in Blissfield, MI.

04

Real Season, Real Stock

Products are available when the farm produces them. When it's gone, it's gone.
