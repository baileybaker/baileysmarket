The farm

One person.
One property.
No shortcuts.

Bailey's Market is a solo operation on a limestone farmhouse property on Silberhorn Hwy in Lenawee County. Every product is grown, processed, and packed here — no middlemen, no outsourced ingredients, no plastic.

Meet the Farm

How we work

Seed to table.
Nothing extra.

The growing season is the business. What gets planted in May ends up in your kitchen by July — and the story of how it got there is written in the soil, not on a marketing deck.

Learn more
01

Zero Plastic

Every product ships in glass, kraft, or compostable packaging. No exceptions.

02

Chemical Free

No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. Managed with cultivation, rotation, and timing.

03

Honestly Labeled

What's on the label is what's in the jar. Grown and produced locally in Blissfield, MI.

04

Real Season, Real Stock

Products are available when the farm produces them. When it's gone, it's gone.
