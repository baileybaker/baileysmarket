For Restaurants & Retail

Wholesale

Fresh, chemical-free product for local kitchens and shops — grown to order and delivered on a schedule that works for you.

Bailey's Market supplies local restaurants, cafes, and shops with fresh herb bundles, specialty produce, and cut flowers grown right here in Blissfield. Everything is harvested to order — no warehouse, no middleman, just what came out of the ground that week.

Standing weekly orders and one-off case orders are both welcome. Availability shifts with the season, so the best way to start is a quick conversation about what you're looking for and when.

Wholesale details, current product availability, and ordering information are coming together here soon. In the meantime, reach out directly and we'll get you sorted.
