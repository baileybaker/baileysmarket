Local Pickup

Order ahead, then pick it up at market or arrange a local handoff. The freshest way to shop the farm.

Local pickup orders are the most direct way to get product from Bailey's Market — placed ahead of time and picked up either at a farmers market stand or through an arranged local handoff in or near Blissfield.

This option works well for fresh items that don't ship — Field Notes produce, fresh-cut Blissful Blooms, and anything else that's best enjoyed the same week it's harvested.

Pickup ordering details and the schedule will be posted here as the season firms up. Check the market schedule for current dates in the meantime.
